Welcome!

Explore how chaos engineering strengthens web services and ensures system reliability.

By: Catherine Bregou, Sam Lengyel, Angel Ortiz Martinez, Ntense Obono, Khizar Qureshi, and Bryan Yang.

Chaos engineering is an approach to testing and improving system reliability by intentionally injecting failures to identify weaknesses before they impact users. By simulating real-world failures, teams can ensure their web services remain resilient under unexpected conditions.
